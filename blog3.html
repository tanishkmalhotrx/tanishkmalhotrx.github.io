AI in Education

Artificial Intelligence has been already widely used over the globe to improve our life, work, health, daily routine, etc. Besides, the solution has great potential in education, and the progress of the technology in this direction gains traction. Today there is a great number of AI-based software, applications, systems created to enhance the teachers’ and students’ experience. However, it’s the very beginning. The solution evolves rapidly. That’s why we believe that the biggest achievements are yet to come.

As the education industry has evolved in step with recent technological advances, new ways of teaching and learning have emerged. This approach is empowering educators with more solutions to better engage learners. From Artificial Intelligence (AI) to immersive learning (process of learning with the usage of a simulated or artificial environment) experiences, the education space is bound to witness a long-lasting impact. As per the research, the use of AI in the education industry will grow by 47.5% through 2025 as the citizens of the world are becoming more adaptive and open to technology tools. The trend is becoming more prevalent in the Asia-Pacific region since the region is home to 60% of the world population. Further, Asia-Pacific has witnessed immense growth in technology, research and development, and many other similar fields. This has led to enhancements in one of the most important sectors in the region i.e. education, thereby opening gates for AI in the sector.

It’s obvious that high technologies change the world and every aspect of being. The education field is not an exception. Though AI hasn’t managed the education system a lot yet, it’s expected that the solution will totally enter the industry in the near future. According to Forbes, it’s forecasted that the e-learning market will be worth $325 billion in 2025. There are numerous ways technology influences the industry, making knowledge and learning more accessible, successful, and flexible. Here we’re going to explore the future of AI technology in education and discover its power for e-learning.
